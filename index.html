Important Information

Booking, Cancellation & Refund Policy

Please read our policies carefully before completing your booking.

1 Booking Confirmation

  • All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation.
  • A booking is considered confirmed only after full payment has been successfully received.
  • Customers are responsible for providing accurate passenger and contact information during the booking process.

2 Cancellation Policy

  • Customers may cancel their booking at any time before the scheduled departure date.
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted through our official communication channels.

3 Refund Policy

  • Approved cancellations are eligible for a 50% refund of the total booking amount.
  • The remaining 50% is non-refundable and covers administrative, processing, and reservation costs.
  • Refunds will be processed using the original payment method whenever possible.
  • Refund processing may take 5–15 business days, depending on the payment provider or bank.

4 No-Show Policy

  • Passengers who fail to appear for their scheduled trip without prior cancellation will be considered a "No-Show."
  • No refunds will be issued for No-Show bookings.

5 Schedule Changes

  • We reserve the right to modify schedules, routes, or services due to weather conditions, operational requirements, safety concerns, or other unforeseen circumstances.
  • In the event of a cancellation initiated by our company, customers may be offered a rescheduled trip or a refund in accordance with applicable regulations.

6 Customer Responsibility

  • Passengers must arrive at the designated boarding area at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • Valid identification may be required prior to boarding.
  • Failure to comply with boarding requirements may result in denied boarding without refund.

7 Contact Information

  • For booking modifications, cancellations, or refund requests, please contact our customer support team through our official website, email, or customer service channels.
By completing a booking, the customer acknowledges and agrees to this Booking, Cancellation, and Refund Policy.
